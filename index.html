What is iTerpeter

iTerpeter uses the AR (Augmented Reality) glasses to make a portable interpreter for the deaf students can use in classes at all times. The student will wear the glasses and while the instructor is speaking and explaining the lecture, through the glasses it will show an interpretation of the speech in sign language using a 3D model. iTerpeter has application used by people to translate sign language to speech to ease the communicate with deaf students .
